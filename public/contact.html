This is the best way to reach us. We read every message and aim to reply within a few business days.

What to include

To help us respond quickly, please add as much of the following as applies:

  • Bug report — what you did, what you expected, and what happened (a screenshot helps).
  • Data correction — the repository link and the date / ranking that looks wrong.
  • Feature idea — what you're trying to do and why the current site falls short.
  • Partnership or press — a short note about what you have in mind.

Corrections & takedowns

Ranking data is sourced from the public github.com/trending page and the Internet Archive's public snapshots. If you are the rights holder for a repository and want a listing adjusted or removed, email us with the repository link and details, and we'll handle it promptly.
