Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

We respect your privacy. This is a static website that requires no sign-up, collects no personal account information, and does not ask for any personally identifying details. This policy explains what data the site touches when you visit and how third-party services may process data.

1. Information we collect

This site does not maintain a backend user database. To remember your preferences, we store settings such as your language choice in your browser's localStorage. That data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2. Third-party ads & cookies

This site may display ads served by Google AdSense. Third-party ad vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site and across the web.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this and other sites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings, or manage third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info.

3. Analytics

This site is hosted on a content delivery network (such as Cloudflare Pages), whose servers may record standard access logs (e.g. IP address, browser type, request time) for security and performance. That data is handled by the hosting provider under its own privacy policy.

4. External links

The open-source projects listed here link to external sites such as GitHub. Once you click through you leave this site, and we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites — please review their respective privacy policies.

5. Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general developer audience, is not directed at children under 13, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; when we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach us via the contact page.
